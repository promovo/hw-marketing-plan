Combating late buying

Tier-based pricing
Early bird → Standard → Final. Price rises visibly as each tier sells. Rewards early action.
Friends-first pre-sale
24-hr community drop before public on-sale. Encourages group bookings and locks in early revenue.
Payment plans
Spread cost over 3–6 months. Directly addresses cost-of-living hesitation. Essential for big shows and Corfu.
Final 14-day countdown
Daily posts: remaining ticket count, lineup reveals, crowd testimonials. Creates sustained urgency in the close window.
